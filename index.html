About Me

I am a Junior Penetration Tester & PHP Developer

My journey into cybersecurity and backend development began with a relentless curiosity to understand how systems work and, more importantly, how they can be broken and secured. Currently training with DEPI, I specialize in vulnerability assessment, ethical hacking, and secure coding practices.

I have hands-on experience with industry-standard tools like Burp Suite, Wireshark, and Metasploit. My participation in multiple CTF competitions and security labs has honed my ability to identify and exploit vulnerabilities, as well as develop robust defenses. For instance, in the ECPC, my team successfully solved over 10 algorithmic problems under strict time limits, demonstrating strong problem-solving and teamwork skills. As a Cyber Security Core Team member at GDSC Benha, I supported technical bootcamps, detecting and mitigating over 15 simulated vulnerabilities (XSS, SQLi, weak passwords) in lab environments.

Beyond security, I enjoy developing secure, real-world systems using PHP, MySQL, and JavaScript, always with an eye towards building scalable and resilient applications. I am passionate about continuous learning and applying my skills to create a more secure digital world.
